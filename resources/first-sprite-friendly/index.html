Make Your First Sprite Say Hello

spriteslooksevents

Kid-friendly walkthrough for adding a sprite, dressing it up, and making it talk when the green flag waves.

Steps

  1. Pick a buddy: Click the blue cat button to add a sprite. Choose any character that makes you smile, or draw your own in the Paint option.
  2. Give them style: Hop into the Costumes tab and color in outfits or switch costumes. Think superhero capes, lab coats, or SDG flair.
  3. Teach a greeting: In the Code tab, drag `when green flag clicked` and snap a `say [Hello DG!] for (2) seconds` block underneath. Your sprite now talks when the flag starts the story.
  4. Add a wiggle: Slide in `move (10) steps` and `move (-10) steps` blocks to create a tiny dance. You can add a `wait (0.5) seconds` in between for rhythm.
  5. Show it off: Click the green flag and watch your sprite wave hello. Ready for more? Add a key press block so it tells a joke when you press space!
