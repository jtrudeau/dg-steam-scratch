Setting Up Your Child's Scratch Account

Because your child is under 16, Scratch requires a parent or guardian to confirm their account by email. This guide walks you through the full process using your child's EMSB school email.

What You Need

  • Your child's EMSB email address (e.g. username@edu.emsb.qc.ca)
  • Your child's EMSB password (used to log into ePortal)
  • A computer with internet access
  • About 10 minutes

Part A — Create the Scratch Account

  1. Step 1

    Go to scratch.mit.edu

    Open scratch.mit.edu in your browser and click the Join Scratch button in the top-right corner.

  2. Step 2

    Choose a username and password

    Do not use your child's real name. Pick a school-safe username such as dg-rocket-17 or dg-star-42. Then choose a password your child can remember. Type it twice to confirm and click Next.

  3. Step 3

    Select country and birthday

    Select Canada and your child's birth month and year. Click Next. You can skip the newsletter.

  4. Step 4

    Enter your child's EMSB email

    Type your child's full EMSB email address (e.g. username@edu.emsb.qc.ca). This is where Scratch will send the confirmation email. Click Create Your Account.

  5. Step 5

    Record the account information

    Write down the username and password somewhere secure (notebook, password manager, or a note kept at home). Your child will need these every time they log in to Scratch at school.

Part B — Confirm the Account via EMSB Email

Scratch sends a confirmation email to the address you entered. For under-16 accounts, a parent or guardian must click the link in that email. Since the email goes to the EMSB address, you'll access it through the EMSB ePortal.

  1. Step 6

    Go to emsb.qc.ca

    Open emsb.qc.ca and click ePortal in the top-right area of the page. Use your child's EMSB email and password here; this is the same account used for other school logins, including Chromebooks and Google Classroom.

  2. Step 7

    Sign in to ePortal

    Enter your child's EMSB email address and EMSB password, then sign in.

  3. Step 8

    Open My WebMail

    Once logged in, find and click the My WebMail button (Outlook icon). This opens the school email inbox.

  4. Step 9

    Find the Scratch confirmation email

    Look for an email from Scratch with a subject like "Confirm your email", "Please verify your email address", or "Your Child is Ready to Create with Scratch".

    Check the Junk / Spam folder if you don't see it in the inbox — it often ends up there.

  5. Step 10

    Click the confirmation link

    Open the email and click the Confirm my email address or Yes, I give permission button/link. Clicking the link will confirm the email and take you back to the Scratch website. This completes account setup.

Confirmation emails expire!

If the link no longer works, don't worry. Just go to scratch.mit.edu, sign in with the username and password you created, and Scratch will give you the option to resend the confirmation email. Then check EMSB WebMail again.

All Done!

Your child's Scratch account is now fully set up and confirmed. They can sign in at scratch.mit.edu using the username and password you recorded. Make sure they bring their login info to class!
