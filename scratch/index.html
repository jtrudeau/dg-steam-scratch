Input Patterns

Build reliable keyboard and mouse controls that feel responsive.

  • Map keys: Choose clear controls and document them on screen.
  • Hold vs tap: Use forever loops for hold controls, events for taps.
  • Debounce: Add short waits to prevent accidental repeats.
