Facilitator Notes

Teacher Goal

Help teams move from sustainability problem framing to feasible Scratch project concepts.

Artifacts

  • team information sheet
  • scratch idea list
  • reflection note

Flow

  1. Team setup (5 min)

    Capture team name and members so everyone has a clear role in the brainstorm.

  2. Business idea framing (10 min)

    Define the problem and explain how the idea helps people, the planet, or both.

  3. Scratch concept brainstorm (15 min)

    Generate at least two possible Scratch formats (game, story, simulation, or interactive message).

  4. Scratch basics exploration (15 min)

    Explore core Scratch features and identify which ones match the team idea.

  5. Reflection and close (5 min)

    Capture one insight about how Scratch can communicate the business idea.

Teacher Moves

  • Keep students focused on what the project should communicate before discussing implementation details.
  • Use prompts that connect problem, audience, and sustainability impact.
  • Remind teams that today is about exploration, not finishing a complete build.
