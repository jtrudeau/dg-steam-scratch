Meeting 3 · discovery

Project Discovery: Sustainable Engineering

Student Objective

Explore UN Sustainable Development Goals and brainstorm Scratch project ideas.

Teacher Goal

Facilitate research into SDGs and guide teams toward feasible project scopes.

Scratch Focus

IdeationResearch

Artifacts

  • Brainstorming Map
  • Project Pitch

Flow

  1. SDG Deep Dive (15 min)

    Review the 17 UN SDGs. Which goals match your Sustainable Business idea? Pick the top 2-3.

  2. Engineering Connection (20 min)

    Look at your business idea through an Engineering lens. Is there a physical product, machine, or system involved? What technical problem does it solve?

  3. Scratch Prototype (15 min)

    How can Scratch show this? Brainstorm a simulation of your machine, an interactive model of your product, or a user journey story.

  4. Feasibility Check (10 min)

    Reality check: Can we code a working prototype of this concept in Scratch, or do we need to simplify?

Teacher Moves

  • Encourage 'Moonshot' ideas first, then help ground them in Scratch capabilities.
  • Ensure every student voices an opinion on the chosen SDG.
