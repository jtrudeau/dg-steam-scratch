Scratch Setup Student Handout

Before you start

  • Use only school-safe usernames (no real names). Example: dg-rocket-17.
  • Have your EMSB email + password ready.
  • Have your notebook open to write your username/password.

Go to Scratch and create your account

  • [ ] Open scratch.mit.edu in a new tab and click Join.
  • [ ] Username: school-safe handle (no real names).
  • [ ] Password: something you can remember; confirm it.
  • [ ] Email: your EMSB email; skip newsletter.

EMSB WebMail

  • [ ] Go to emsb.qc.ca and click ePortal.
  • [ ] Sign in with your EMSB email + password.
  • [ ] Click My WebMail and confirm you can see your inbox.

Verify + record

  • [ ] Open EMSB WebMail and find the Scratch email.
  • [ ] Click Confirm my email address.
  • [ ] Sign out/in to Scratch to verify.
  • [ ] Write your Scratch username + password in your notebook.
  • [ ] Log out of EMSB WebMail so the next teammate can use it.

First project

  • [ ] Go to scratch.mit.edu/ideas.
  • [ ] Watch the first video: how to create a sprite.
  • [ ] Add a sprite in your own project and try the steps from the video.
  • [ ] Save your project.
